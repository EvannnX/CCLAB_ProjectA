Project Introduction // SPARK

Entity Profile

Generative Creature’s name: SPARK
Scientific Name: Spark-like organisms
Habitat: Lighting Storm
Discovered by/at: A storm with thunder and lightning
Appearance: Unstable lightning sparks
Family: Unknown

Key Characteristics

  • Nervous Movement: It constantly jitters and shakes like a loose wire connection.
  • Autonomous Drifting: When left alone, it navigates its environment smoothly using Perlin noise.
  • Magnetic Tethering: It gets highly attracted to foreign objects like a cursor if they enter its central territory.
  • Boundary Avoidance: If pulled too close to the edge of its environment, it will break the connection and escape back into autonomous drifting.
  • Breathing Edges: Its body is not solid. It is made of jagged, electric lines that expand and contract rhythmically over time.

Research Data

Spark is a nervous, electricity-based lifeform living in a digital thunderstorm. It drifts aimlessly until a foreign object enters its central territory. It gets magnetically attracted to the cursor, but if pushed too close to the environment's edge, it breaks the connection and escapes into the storm.

Interaction Protocol

You interact with Spark using your mouse. When your cursor enters the central safe zone, Spark gets magnetically attracted and follows it while jittering nervously. If you drag it too close to the screen edge, the connection snaps and it escapes to drift autonomously in the storm.
